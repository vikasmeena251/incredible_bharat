GULMARG

Gulmarg is a town in the Baramula district of Jammu and Kashmir, the Indian state. It is a very popular hill station too. The place is well known for skiing. The place is fifty two kilometers away from Srinagar. The elevation of the place is at an average 2,690 m. The place served as the resort for many renowned kings in the history of India like Jahangir and Yousuf Shah Chak who were the frequent visitor of this place. DAL LAKE

Dal Lake is among the largest lakes in the world. It is a lake near Tota Rani and is above 1,775 m above the sea level. This lake is also known as the jewel in the crown of Jammu and Kashmir. The lake is a major tourist attraction in Srinagar and is also famous for many recreational activities. The lake is also used for many commercial uses like fishing and also for water plant harvesting. The shore line is beautified by many Mughal era gardens, houseboats, hotels and many beautiful parks. When the winters come the temperature reaches to -11 degree Celsius. This low temperature freezes the lake.
